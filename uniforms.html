Introducing the Arbor School's Sustainable Uniform

  • As we continue to develop and embed sustainability as a whole-school approach, we are excited to have launched a 100% sustainable, organic, and fair-trade uniform to our community, through our partnership with Kapes.

  • Kapes, are the only providers of sustainable uniform in the UAE. All products are made in ethically certified factories that guarantee employee welfare whilst paying fair wages, just one of the reasons we are proud to be partnered with them.

  • This partnership is more than just helping reduce the environmental impact of our uniforms. We want to use school uniforms as a tool for positive change in the world. Therefore, for every student at Arbor that purchases a Kapes uniform, we will give a free uniform to a child in need in a developing country. Making our school uniforms good for the students wearing them, good for the people making them and good for the planet we all live on.

HOW TO ORDER?

  • Uniforms can be ordered via our uniform provider Kapes’ website. To place your uniform order, please visit shopkapes.com and use the code provided to you by Arbor Parent Relations.

  • We invite anyone wishing to try on items before buying to visit the fitting room during school working hours. The fitting room will remain at your disposal throughout the academic year.

  • Kapes customer support is available through live chat on www.shopkapes.com, phone support and email arbor@shopkapes.com for your questions. For school’s uniform expectations, please refer to the link here .
